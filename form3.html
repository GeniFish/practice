Fans' Feedback for Inateck Charger

Hi fans, thanks for joining in #Inateck111WChargerTest on our Facebook! Here we’d like to specify the details about how to test our CC03001 charger, and how to provide your feedback to us. make sure to test and fill in this form within one month after you receive our CC03001.

How to test?

● Test the charging time from 1% to 100% for your mobile phone via USB-C or USB-A port of CC03001. If your phone has a USB-C, please do test USB-C port charging instead of USB-A.

● If you have a tablet or laptop with USB-C port, please also test the charging time from 1% to 100% via USB-C of CC03001.

● After 1-2 weeks of usage, please tell us your feedback about your using experience & advice below.
